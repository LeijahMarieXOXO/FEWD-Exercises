Lord of the Rings: Return of the King

This movie is about the fellowships continued journey to Mount Doom to destroy the "One Ring" and save the world from Sauron's Orcish army. It focuses on Sam and Frodo's perilous journey, Aragorn's rise to kingship, and the final battle for Middle-earth. It also delves into Gollum's backstory and his deterioration as Smeagol to become Gollum.

  • Elijah Woods as Frodo
  • Sean Astin as Samwise
  • Ian McKellen as Gandalf
  • Viggo Mortensen as Aragorn
  • Orlando Bloom as Legolas
  • John Rhys-Davies as Gimli
  • Andy Serkis as Gollum
  • Dominic Monaghan as Merry
  • Billy Boyd as Pippin
